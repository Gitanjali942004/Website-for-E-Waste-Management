Donate E-Waste

Why Donate E-Waste?

Electronic products are made up metals, plastics, and glass, all of which require energy to mine and manufacture. Donating or recycling consumer electronics conserves our natural resources and avoids air and water pollution. By donating your e-waste, you can contribute to reducing the environmental impact and promote recycling.



What Can You Donate?
  • Old computers and laptops
  • Smartphones and tablets
  • Printers and scanners
  • TVs and monitors
  • Electronic accessories (keyboards, mice, cables, etc.)
